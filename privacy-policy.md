Privacy Policy
Last Updated: March 21, 2026

1. Overview
We believe that your data belongs to you. This app is designed to be private by design. It operates as a "local-only" application, meaning all data you create, input, or save remains on your device at all times.

2. Data Collection & Usage
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

No Personal Information: We do not ask for your name, email, or phone number.

No Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the app or use third-party analytics cookies.

No Cloud Sync: By default, your data is never uploaded to a cloud database or shared with us.

3. Local Storage
The app utilizes local storage (such as SQLite, SharedPreferences, or IndexedDB) to save your progress and settings directly on your hardware.

This data is stored within the app's private directory.

We have no technical means of accessing this data.

If you delete the app, the local data is typically deleted by your operating system.

4. Third-Party Services
Because the app runs locally, there are no third-party SDKs (like Facebook or Google Analytics) integrated into the core experience that would scrape your data.

Note: If you use the app to export a file or share content via other apps on your device, the privacy policies of those specific apps will apply once the data leaves our environment.

5. Security
While we do not handle your data on our servers, the security of the data on your device depends on your own device's security settings (e.g., passcodes, biometrics, or encryption). We recommend keeping your operating system updated to ensure your local storage remains secure.

6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy if we add new features. However, our commitment to your privacy will remain the same: if we ever decide to implement cloud features, they will be opt-in only.
